Lazy Toad
Studios
A one-person maker studio based in Kobe, Japan.

Lazy Toad Studios makes hand-crafted objects that sit somewhere between electronics, woodworking, and whatever else seems interesting that week.

Projects range from custom smartwatches and handmade bags to card games and furniture — all built with hand tools and a healthy disregard for doing things the easy way.

This site is the shop front, the gallery, and the ongoing record of what's being made.
